Melatonin Dreams (BoyWithUke Album)


Melatonin Dreams is the debut studio album by American alt-pop artist BoyWithUke, released independently on January 22, 2021. The 13-track album delves into themes of depression, anxiety, and suicidal thoughts, while also offering glimmers of hope amidst the darkness. Entirely self-written, produced, and recorded, the album showcases BoyWithUke's introspective songwriting and lo-fi production style. Notably, all songs were initially released as singles on SoundCloud before being compiled into the album. The album's tracklist includes "Blurry Nights," "Letter for My Bed," and "Route 9.""
