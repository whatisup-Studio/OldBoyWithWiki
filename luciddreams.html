Lucid Dreams (BoyWithUke Album)


Lucid Dreams is the fourth studio album by American alt-pop artist BoyWithUke, released on October 6, 2023, via Mercury Records/Republic Records. The 14-track album delves into themes of personal growth, trauma, and self-discovery, blending elements of alternative, pop, and electronic music. BoyWithUke wrote, recorded, and produced most of the album in a three-week period, marking a deeply introspective chapter in his musical journey. The album includes singles such as "Migraine," "Rockstar," and "Trauma," the latter of which is noted as his most personal song to date.
