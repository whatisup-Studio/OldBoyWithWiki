Antisocial (BoyWithUke EP)


Antisocial is an extended play (EP) by American alt-pop artist BoyWithUke, released on February 24, 2023, through Mercury Records and Republic Records. The three-track EP includes "Rockstar," "idtwcbf (friends)," and "Nosedive," all of which were previously featured in Volume 4 of his Minute-Long Songs series. "Rockstar" was released as a single two weeks prior to the EP's release. The EP was released between his albums Serotonin Dreams and Lucid Dreams, but "idtwcbf (friends)" and "Nosedive" were not included in Lucid Dreams. Antisocial likely replaced a scrapped 2022 EP.
