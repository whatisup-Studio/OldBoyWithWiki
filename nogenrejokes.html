No genre/jokes (BWU SoundCloud Mixtape/EP)


No genre/jokes is an independently released EP by American alt-pop artist BoyWithUke, made available exclusively on SoundCloud on September 3, 2020.The 11-track project showcases BoyWithUke's early experimentation with lo-fi production, ukulele-driven melodies, and introspective lyricism, blending humor and emotional vulnerability with a playful, genre-defying tone; Entirely self-written and produced by Charley Yang, the EP reflects his creative spontaneity and DIY approach during the early stages of his music career. The original tracklist included: "Sunday" "Poser Boy" "Alien" "Dead a Little Sooner" "Poopy Snow" "Orange Juice" "Melancholic" "Your Time's Up" "Never Ever 3" "Vocabulary" "Help My Dentist Is Evil" The track "Vocabulary" was later deleted from the EP after it was found to contain the F-slur, a decision made by BoyWithUke to acknowledge and take responsibility for the use of offensive language in his earlier work. The EP remains a snapshot of his artistic beginnings, valued by fans for its rawness and insight into the evolution of his sound and lyrical sensibilities.
