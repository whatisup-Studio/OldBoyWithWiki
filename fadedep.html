Faded (BoyWithUke EP)


Faded is the second extended play (EP) by American alt-pop artist BoyWithUke, released independently on September 10, 2021. The EP comprises three tracks: "LoveSick," "Toxic," and "Loafers." These songs originated from his Minute-Long Songs series, where he expanded brief compositions into full-length tracks. "Toxic" gained significant attention, going viral on TikTok and amassing over 1.2 billion streams worldwide. The EP showcases BoyWithUke's signature lo-fi production and introspective songwriting, contributing to his rise in the alt-pop scene.
